Lend-A-Shoulder

Volunteer program for employees to develop local communities

Lend-A-Shoulder is our employee volunteering program that allows our colleagues to extend their time and skills towards social causes they believe in. It offers a direction to our colleagues who are keen on community development and being a catalyst for social change in their own little ways. We are delighted that our colleagues , through the Lend-A-Shoulder program, have been able to support more than 10,000 people so far.

Entrepreneurship & Livelihood

Opportunities to support the passions of marginalized people

We at IDFC FIRST Bank have created livelihood development programs that will enable people to develop skills and take advantage of opportunities in the marketplace. This will help this build a quality life. Through our programs, we have been able to assist more than 75,000 people.

Education

Financial aid for aspiring students

Access to quality education is the bare minimum that every aspiring student must get. We provide the necessary financial aid to enable students to avail higher education programs. To date, we have been able to successfully support the education goals of more than 17,000 students.

Health & Sanitation

Building sanitized communities through relevant knowledge

Sanitation and sustainability help build clean, healthy communities. We have partnered with Stree Mukti Sanghatana and the Municipal Corporation of Greater Mumbai to create awareness about best practices in solid and waste management that will help reduce health risks. So far, we have managed to support 27,500 people through this program.

Women Empowerment

Create employment opportunities for women

Women's Empowerment is not just a cause for us. We truly believe in helping women become financially independent. We offer women from both rural and urban slums livelihood opportunities, that empower them financially. Through our programs, we have helped more than 3500 women step out and take charge.

COVID-19 Programme

Relief programs for the Ecomonically Weaker section

COVID-19 pandemic has changed the way we look at life. It has affected every section of society, but mostly the economically weaker sections. We at IDFC FIRST Bank took the onus of extending support to frontline doctors, nurses, police officers, the local municipal corporation workers and every helping hand. With your support, we have served 40 lakh COVID-19 warriors.
