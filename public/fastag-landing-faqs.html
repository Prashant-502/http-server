FIRSTforward FASTag

IDFC FIRST Bank FASTag is a simple, reloadable tag which enables automatic payment of toll charges & lets you pass through the toll plaza without stopping for the cash transaction. FASTag is linked to a prepaid account from which the applicable toll amount is deducted. read more.

Always keep the adhesive side of the FASTag sticker facing outside

Once the sticker is pasted in, do not try to remove or reposition the same.
