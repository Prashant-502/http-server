Asian Private Bankers Best Private Bank - Digital Innovation & Services 2019

IDFC Bank wins accolades in IDC Digital Transformation Awards 2017. We are pleased to announce that our bank has been recognised as a "Digital Transformation Leader" for “Omni-Experience Innovation” at the IDC Digital Transformation Awards 2017.

This win demonstrates the value of putting client experience at the heart of digital transformation with the help of our partner iGTB by Intellect Design Arena Ltd., the transaction banking and technology specialist.

The award is a true vindication of the innovative digital transformation brought about by BXP:

  • Providing seamless omni channel experience
  • Convenience via various features viz. 2 click payments, document uploads etc
  • Bringing in efficiency in processes resulting improved customer TATs
  • Leveraging on data availability to work out client specific analytics

The IDC Digital Transformation Awards honour the achievements of organisations within the Asia-Pacific region that have successfully planned and executed a digital transformation – of one or multiple areas of their business – through the use of digital and disruptive technologies.

Congratulations to the entire team.
