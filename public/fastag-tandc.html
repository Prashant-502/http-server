The Tag shall be activated subject to approval of the application by the Bank and a minimum amount being loaded on the Tag by the Tag Member. Such funds shall be loaded on the tag after deduction of the applicable charges/fees etc. payable by the Tag Member to IDFC FIRST Bank for availing the Tag.

The tag holder shall be bound to comply with these Terms and Conditions and all the policies stipulated by IDFC FIRST Bank from time to time in relation to the Tag. IDFC FIRST Bank may, at its sole discretion, refuse to accept the Application and to Issue the Tag to the Tag Member.

No interest shall be payable by IDFC FIRST Bank on the amount available/loaded on the Tag. A User ID and password will be provided to the Tag Member to allow him/her to reload the Tag through the Tag Website. The Tag member must not disclose this User ID and password to anyone else.

The Tag Members may load and reload the Tag at Point of Sale locations Kiosks subject to verification of their identity and any other mandatary requirements as prescribed by the Bank from time to time and/or using the payment gateway available to the Tag Members after they log in to Tag Website using the User ID and password provided to them. Limitations regarding minimum amount, maximum amount, annual load limit and frequency of loading and reloading shall be applicable as prescribed by the Bank from time to time. The cash loading/ reloading facility is subject to the relevant applicable fees. Funds loaded on a Tag after activation by IDFC FIRST Bank are immediately available for the Tag Member to use.
